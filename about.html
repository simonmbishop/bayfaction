about

Bay Faction is an indie rock band from Jamaica Plain, Boston, MA.
Forming in 2012, James (vocals, guitar), Connor (drums),
and Kris (Bass)have been writing songs and playing shows in and
out of the Boston for nearly four years. In the fall of 2015, after the
release of their debut self-titled album, they embarked on their first tour,
traveling as far as Nashville, Tennessee. This summer they will be
touring through Canada on their "Canada & US Tour"
(see "tour" page for more details).
